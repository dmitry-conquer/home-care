Home Health Care Services in Pittsburgh, PA

Home health care services, also referred to as “skilled care”, are administered by trained healthcare professionals who bring a wealth of medical expertise to your doorstep. The key word in this term is “health,” as this type of care requires clinical proficiency. This category includes registered nurses, physical therapists, occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, and other skilled caregivers. As such, home healthcare agencies focus on providing specialized medical assistance. While A+ Home Care does not provide home health care services in Pittsburgh, PA, we want to help inform your decision on what is best for you or your loved one.

Services within home health care in Pittsburgh, PA may include:

Wound Care

Expert management of wounds to promote healing and prevent infections. 

Injections

Administering injections as prescribed by a healthcare provider. 

Vital Sign Monitoring:

Regularly checking and recording vital signs like blood pressure, pulse, and temperature. 

Rehabilitation:

Tailored rehabilitation programs to aid recovery from surgery or manage chronic medical conditions. 

Home healthcare agencies in Pittsburgh, PA, typically require a doctor's prescription or referral. Additionally, skilled care often qualifies for coverage through health insurance, making it a cost-effective option for those in need of specialized medical attention in the comfort of their homes.

In-Home Care Services in Pittsburgh, PA

In-home care services refer to non-skilled caregiving that caters to individuals who require assistance with daily living activities, companionship, and general support. While the term “non-skilled” can be misleading, these caregivers strive to provide you or your loved ones with an elevated standard of living and comfort. This category of care does not involve medical procedures or treatments; it instead focuses on enhancing the overall quality of life for clients through assistance with daily needs.

Common in-home care services in Pittsburgh, PA include:

Assistance with Daily Living

Help with tasks such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility.

Medication Reminders:

Ensuring clients take their medications as prescribed.

Light Housekeeping:

Keeping the living environment clean and organized.

Meal Preparation:

Keeping the living environment clean and organized.

Companionship:

Engaging in meaningful conversations, playing games, or accompanying clients on outings.

One of the advantages of non-skilled care is its flexibility.

Clients can tailor their care plans to their unique needs and preferences, making it a highly customizable option. Unlike the skilled care provided by home healthcare agencies, many non-skilled in-home care services in Pittsburgh, PA are usually paid for privately and do not require a doctor's prescription.
